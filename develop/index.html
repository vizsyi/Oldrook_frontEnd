Cards are laid out face down in a grid. Each card has exactly one matching pair somewhere in the grid.

    Flip over two cards:
  • If they match, they will be removed.
  • If they don’t match, they will be faced down again

    • Win by matching all pairs as quickly and with as few moves as possible.
